Prime Areas

Communication and language

Personal, social and emotional development

Physical development

One of the key focus areas of our Curriculum is the development of children’s spoken language, as it is the critical area of development for children. We are aiming to create a language rich environment full of stories, rhymes, songs, quality child-adult interactions, collective play which stimulate children’s speech and language development and create good foundation for their future success in learning.

Specific areas

Literacy

Mathematics

Understanding the world

Expressive arts and design

Specific areas of learning and development provide children with knowledge and skills to flourish in society. At Miracle Gardens we support children to know about and use these Specific areas, either informally as part of daily life or in planned activities, we give children access to the wide scope of shared cultural and intellectual life in modern society, and skills and knowledge to support them in their future learning.
