Be smart, eat smart

Food and drinks

At Miracle Gardens we take the meals of our little ones seriously. We work in partnership with British Nutrition Foundation which provides us with science –based information to support in creating a healthy, sustainable diet for the children in our care.

Every day is an opportunity for us to satisfy your child’s appetite with delicious and nutritious meals and snacks, designed with their needs in mind, with a menu planned to broaden their food experiences.

Most of all our nursery meals are organic and well -balanced.

Breakfast

We all know that breakfast is the most important meal of the day. So, to get our children off to flying start, we offer a variety of non-sugared cereals and wholemeal toast, enough to fill up their bellies and keep a smile on their face till snack time.

Snack


We offer children variety of fruit or slices of vegetables.

Lunch


At Lunch time we like to serve hot, balanced nursery meals that has been cooked on the premises of st Mary's School.

Tea time


At Tea time we provide children with a light snack such as rice pudding, sandwiches accompanied by salad, cheese, variety of yogurts, oat biscuits, fruit.

Throughout the day and at meal times we encourage children to drink fresh water and milk.
What if my child has special dietary requirements?

We understand that children may have very specific food requirements due to allergies, food intolerances, cultural background, or other family preferences. As part of our menu planning process, we work in consultation with all of our families to make sure their individual child’s dietary requirements are met at all times.

Copies of our four week menus and recipes are available at the nursery. Please ask our manager for a copy.
